        Note that $E[X|Y]$ is a function of $y$.
        \begin{align*}
            E[E[X|Y]] &= \sum_{y} E[X|Y] p_Y(y)\\
                     &= \sum_{y} \sum_{x} xp_{X|Y}p_{Y}\\
                     &= \sum_{y}\sum_{x} xp_{X,Y}(x,y)\\
                     &= \sum_{x}x\sum_{y}p_{X,Y}(x,y)\\
                     &= \sum_{x} x p_{X}(x)\\
                     &= E[X]
        \end{align*}
